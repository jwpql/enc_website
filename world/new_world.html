Common Era

Map

Era after the death of the Creator. Also called the Human Age or more rarely, the New World.

The moment the Creator died, fragments of its power, freed from the god, tethered themselves to some of Earth's remaining creations. This unexpected development allowed low-mana life to continue within a fixed radius of these new gods known as Vessels.

Holy Vessels

Also known as New Gods, Holy Vessels are beings who hold the remnants of the Creator's power. They emanate some amount of mana- the force that used to emanate from the planet's core and sustained life. Highly sought-after individuals, as life can only exist in their vicinity. Most are confined to staying at a single town or village, sometimes by contract but often by force. There seems to be no discernable pattern in who was "chosen" to be a god; Holy Vessels can be humans, animals, plants, or even groups of lifeforms.

Notable Vessels include:

  • The Empress
  • Fortune
  • Emek
  • Mela
  • Yue Yuan
  • Sahure
