New World



*This page is a work in progress! There may be information missing.

Era after the Failed Rapture and subsequent death of the Creator. The moment the Creator died, fragments of its power, freed from the god, tethered themselves to some of Earth's remaining creations. This unexpected development allowed low-magic life to continue within a fixed radius of these New Gods.

New Gods

Highly sought-after lifeforms. There seems to be no discernable pattern in who was "chosen" to be a god; New Gods can be humans, animals, plants, or even Old Gods. A large number of New Gods are confined to one region, sometimes by contract but often by force, in order to support human civilizations. Notable New Gods include:

  • The Empress
  • Fortune
  • Emek
  • Mela
  • Yue Yuan
  • Sahure
