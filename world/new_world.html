New World

Map of the New World

A time period also referred to as the Common Era. Era after the death of the Creator. The moment the Creator died, fragments of its power, freed from the god, tethered themselves to some of Earth's remaining creations. This unexpected development allowed low-mana life to continue within a fixed radius of these new gods known as Holy Vessels.

Humans are the main intelligent life of this era.
