Holy Vessels

Map of the New World

Also known as New Gods, they are beings who hold the remnants of the Creator's power following its death in year 0. They emanate some amount of mana- the force that used to emanate from the planet's core and sustained life. Highly sought-after individuals, as life can only exist in their vicinity. Without mana, most humans will develop sunsickness and die painfully within a year. Thus, most Holy Vessels confined to staying at a single town or village, sometimes by contract but often by force. There seems to be no discernable pattern in who was "chosen" to be a god; Holy Vessels can be humans, animals, plants, or even groups of lifeforms.

Notable Vessels include:

  • Emek
  • The Empress
  • Fortune
  • Mela
  • Sahure
  • Vermolen
  • Yue Yuan
