Iterations

Periods of time in the Old World. Defined by the type of dominant lifeform on Earth at the time. One can think of it like different periods in an artist's career.

List of Known Iterations

  • Incorporeal (?? - -10,000)
    • Incorporeal beings made of pure mana. The first beings to have free will.
    • Includes the first and last creations incapable of death. The Earth learns from its mistakes.
  • Amorphous (-10,000 - 9,000)
    • Beings that had solid, albeit changing bodies. They deformed their body using mana to move, eat, and build.
  • Radial (-9,000 - -8,500)
  • (add more)
  • Humanoid (-500 - 0)
    • Requires little mana to operate. In exchange, they are weaker and shorter-lived than previous iterations.
