Basileios is the crown prince of the Aetesian Empire who is prophesied to become a Holy Vessel. He is widely assumed to be the future successor of his mother, the Empress.

A carefree and empathetic young man who revels in luxury and novelty. He is a chronic rule breaker whose clover tends and charm lets him get away with most things. He fears eternity more than anything else.
