Miscellaneous characters

Characters that have yet to be added or have insufficient information to warrant a page.

  • Empress- Holy Vessel, ruler of Aetesian Empire, Basil's mother. Emotionless, expressionless, the reason Basil is so afraid of becoming a god.
  • Aestesian diplomat- Empress' right hand (man? woman?), recuited new gods for empire.
  • An Tai- Aeon, co-ruler of Taiyue with Yuan. Nicer and more sociable than Yuan, took care of diplomacy stuff. More science inclined but also didn't believe in separating science and art.
  • Mountain god cult leader- Vessel that looks like a mountain but the tunnels are weirdly fleshy and moist. Mountain range isolates Heartglade (village) from everywhere else. It demands human sacrifices. Uses human vessels to communicate with people.
  • Kit- teenager chosen to be Mountain God's successor (vessel) who runs away out of fear and joins a theater. Charming, loves attention, cripplingly insecure and wracked with guilt.
  • Moon- Vessel that travels around in a boat for delivery and trading purposes. Earned nickname because their constant route around the continent is like the moon's orbit.
  • Southern Stars- Vessel that is a school of glowing white fish. Fish die if you take them out of the group. Predictable migration cycles.
