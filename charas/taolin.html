One of the most well-known artists in Taiyue. Their art perfectly captures traditional ideals- it is flowery and delicate, with themes that are abstract and intangible. They has transcended worldly matters, or so they believe.

The Tao family has a long history in the Taiyue art scene, most notably having founded of the Tao Institute for the Arts. Since then, the skill and fame of the family have varied, though their initial fortune prevented any of them from falling into true poverty. Tao Lin was born at a high point, with both of their parents being renowned craftsmen. They are a young and promising successor, drawing inspiration from the ancient traditions Taiyue was birthed from.

Tao Lin themself is a polite, mild individual with a short temper they do their best to hide. They aim to embody old ideals of modesty and humility, though they are slightly more humanist than their predecessors.
