Personality

Calm, gentle, and philosophical, Fortune seems like the ideal of a forest guardian. She was told by her creator to protect the grove, and fulfills this task the best she can, playing the part of a wise, unbiased idol. In reality, she only has as much knowledge as she has seen and read and inferred. She has a vague sense of identity, lacking likes, dislikes, and hobbies outside of being a New God. She tends to take a passive role in life. She fears looking too deep into her identity may reveal that she is not fit for the role of god, depriving her of the only place she has ever known and belonged to.

Fortune is naturally curious; this can occasionally override her sense of responsibility. She studies and reads about nature for the sake the townspeople, but enjoys it greatly. She wants badly to know about her own origins.
