Year
???The Great Creator makes the first lifeform, marking the start of the Old World.
-10,000Earliest sign of life- a wisp with no physical body and the faintest traces of neural responses. It is unknown if it was once sentient.
-10,000River Watchers are created and stationed at various points along the Aortic River to find and lifeforms that flow from the ground.
-9,000Earliest evidence of civilization- the Artrium, uncovered by the source of the Aortic River.
-2,000First record of organisms capable of reproduction. Earth no longer has to hand craft every creature.
-900Taiyue is established
-1The death of the Great Creator is prophesized. A slow period of decline begins, as the Earth becomes increasingly unable to support life.
0The Great Rapture- the Great Creator's moment of Death- occurs. New Gods are created from its dissipating power, marking the start of the New World.
800The New Prophecy is issued, predicting the ascension of a New God.
